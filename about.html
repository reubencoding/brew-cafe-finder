About BREW

Discover the story behind Chennai's premier spot guide

Our Mission

BREW is dedicated to helping coffee lovers and adventurers discover the best spots across Chennai. We believe that every cup tells a story, and every place has a unique vibe worth experiencing.

What We Do

We curate handpicked locations—from cozy corners and garden retreats to specialty roasteries and work-friendly spaces. Each spot is carefully reviewed and updated to ensure you have the most authentic experience.

Community Driven

BREW thrives on community contributions. Users can write reviews, save favorites, and book tables directly through the platform. Together, we're building a living guide that evolves with Chennai's vibrant scene.

Technology

Built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, powered by Firebase for seamless user authentication, real-time data, and offline support. No frameworks, just clean code and great coffee.
